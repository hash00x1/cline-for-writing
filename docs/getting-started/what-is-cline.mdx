---
title: "What is Cline Writer?"
description: "An introduction to Cline Writer, your AI-powered writing assistant in VS Code."
---

Cline Writer is an AI writing assistant that integrates with Microsoft Visual Studio Code. It provides an intelligent interface between your writing environment and language models, facilitating academic research, screenwriting, and novel writing. It can read/write files, conduct research using your web browser, and expand its capabilities with Model Context Protocol servers.

What makes Cline Writer distinctive is its thoughtful approach to writing projects and extensive research integration capabilities. Rather than simply generating text snippets, Cline Writer collaborates with writers by planning projects step-by-step in PLAN mode, conducting thorough research, maintaining awareness of your entire writing project through the memory_bank system, and requiring explicit approval for all changes. It can understand complex writing projects, accelerate research and drafting for writers, and connect with hundreds of tools through its Model Context Protocol Marketplace, enabling everything from academic citation management to screenplay formatting—all through natural language commands.
